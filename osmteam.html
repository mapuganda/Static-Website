The Board of Directors

The Board of Directors actively contributes to the development and growth of OpenStreetMap Uganda. They work tirelessly to ensure that the organization remains a leading force in promoting open mapping, fostering collaboration, and unlocking the power of geospatial data for the benefit of all involved.The primary objective of the Board of Directors is to support OpenStreetMap Uganda in achieving its mission: to create and maintain a comprehensive, freely available map of Uganda that is accessible to all.
